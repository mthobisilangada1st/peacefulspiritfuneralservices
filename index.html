Who We Are

Structured, compassionate care in every community we serve.

Peaceful Spirit Funeral Services is a professional and compassionate provider of funeral services, operating from Bulawayo with additional service presence in Mataga Business Centre (Mberengwa) and Zvishavane. This reach allows families across multiple communities to access reliable funeral support.

Built on strong values of respect, innovation and professionalism, we support families, organisations and government departments during times of loss with dignity and structured planning — combining modern funeral planning with dignified service delivery, so every family receives respectful, well-organised support.

How We Support Families

We manage funeral arrangements with care, organisation and respect — clearly explaining every option and coordinating services according to family wishes and cultural expectations.

We work closely with families, churches, burial societies, community representatives and government departments so that arrangements run smoothly, from planning and body handling to chapel services, transport coordination and repatriation, local or international.
